Your Questions, Answered.

Here are some of the most common questions we get about Yodha, our Digital Employee Platform. If you have other questions, feel free to reach out to our team.

Yodha is a Digital Employee Platform that allows you to hire specialized AI agents to run critical business functions. Instead of using fragmented SaaS tools, you get a cohesive AI workforce that you can manage like real employees.

Most AI companies build single-use tools that solve tasks. Yodha builds a workforce that solves entire functions. Other AI acts as an assistant; a Yodha is a digital employee. SaaS gives you software; Yodha gives you a team.

You can hire role-specific AI agents like an AI SEO Specialist, an AI Recruiter, an AI Social Media Manager, and an AI Operations Manager. We are constantly adding new roles to the platform.

You manage your Yodhas through a simple dashboard. You can onboard them, assign them roles and tasks, train them on your company culture, and measure their performance with clear metrics, just like you would with a human team.
