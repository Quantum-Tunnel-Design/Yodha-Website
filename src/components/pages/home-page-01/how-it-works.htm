How it works

Your AI Workforce, Ready in
3 Simple Steps.

1. Hire & Train Your Agent

Select a role-specific AI agent and train it on your brand voice, culture, and KPIs. It's like onboarding, but faster.

2. Delegate Work & Set Goals

Assign tasks and projects to your AI employees just like you would with a human team. Set clear goals and let your workforce execute.

3. Monitor Performance & Scale

Track your agents' performance on a simple dashboard. As your company grows, you can easily add more AI employees to your team.
